## Tiles  
Categorized based on relevant tile mechanics and effects in no particular order. Tiles might fall under multiple categorizes.
Some of these are remixes, which either improve the function or aesthetics of a tile. Non-text effect tiles were specificly choosen. Effect tiles will have rules cards unless otherwise specifed.
Some tiles may have an (Author) tag in its name due to duplicate names or remixes.
Some tiles may have a (Pack) tag in it's name. If a pack doesn't recommend all tiles in it, it will specify the ones recommended.
There are hundred of tiles to choose from! If you're looking for more, head over to the [Tile Table](https://cadeluca.github.io/hextraction-catalog/tile_table/).
If you don't like a particular tile or category, feel free to not print them. It's your set afterall!
